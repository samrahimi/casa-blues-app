About Casa Blues

Why Stay With Us?

Our property is pet-friendly and perfect for guests traveling with furry companions. Enjoy the vibrant gardens, rustic charm, and tranquil courtyards.

Each unit has a fully equipped kitchen, and we are in the heart of historic El Cerillo; this is part of the original colonial town dating back nearly 500 years, and is just steps away from the famous "Old Market" where you can find fresh ingredients to suit every taste. Your unit comes with a traditional fireplace, and we provide firewood for cozy evenings.

Discover the cultural richness of San Cristobal de las Casas: the Casa is a short walk from artisans, restaurants, cathedrals, and the famous "Walking Street" (Andador).
